Creating a basic webpage on Github:

1) Create an account on Github. Make sure you add your name and photograph.
2) Using VSCode or other tool, make a basic HTML page called index.html that displays your photo and your name, with content in the following format, with "Wanda" replaced with your own name.:



    
 Wanda's Portfolio

3) Create a repository on Github titled with your username like so: username.github.io (eg scarletwitch.github.io)
4) Upload index.html and your photo to your repository.
5) Go to https://username.github.io (like my page https://theweather.github.io) and see if the page displays the way you expect.
